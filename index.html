About Me

My name is Jake. I’m a 2019 graduate of the University of Texas at El Paso with a degree in International Business, and I’m returning to a life that values recovery, reflection, writing, and meaningful growth. I’m still building as a developer, but my focus is broader now: creating with intention, taking care of myself, and learning how to live and work in a way that feels honest and sustainable. Outside of technology, I’m drawn to travel, culture, and the quiet discipline of self-development.
