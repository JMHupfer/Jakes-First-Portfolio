About Me

My name is Jake. I'm a 2019 graduate of the University of Texas at El Paso with a degree in International Business, and I'm returning to software development after time away from the field. I'm currently working in retail while rebuilding my skills through independent projects — building for the fun of it, and to stay sharp for where tech (especially AI) is heading. I completed a full-stack bootcamp in 2022 and I'm picking back up where I left off: writing code, shipping projects, and growing as a developer. When I'm not coding, I love travel and learning about other cultures — I studied abroad in Leipzig, Germany, and spent time teaching in Shenzhen, China.
